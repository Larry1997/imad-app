Article on Persian Cats

By: Larsen Dsouza

Wednesday, 2 August

Persian kittens were first introduced to Europe during the 16th century by traders traveling from Turkey. Due to their beautiful long hair and docile temperament, the breed rapidly became a favorite throughout Europe and North America. These beautiful animals make perfect pets, as they are extremely loyal and remain incredibly playful throughout their lives, even in their mature years. Since there are numerous Persian cat breeders in the United States, adoption of Persian cats must be a careful one.
